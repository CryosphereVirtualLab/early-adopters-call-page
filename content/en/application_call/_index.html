Call announcement

During fall/winter period CVL will announce a public Early Adopters call. The main purpose is to collect feedback from users on the user experience and functionality of the system.
We will ask you to submit proposals for small research projects where CVL can provide an additional value. Selected applications will receive support from the European Space Agency.

{{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}}

Who can apply?

Anyone can use CVL, but the call is meant mostly for early career scientists. If you are a Master or a PhD student, or a postdoc, then watch this space!

How to apply?

For more information about how to apply please see our documentation page.
